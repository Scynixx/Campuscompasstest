January 31, 2011 marks another milestone for STI

As it officially inaugurates Naga's very own STI Academic Center, a prime hub of real-life education, located at the Central Business District II of Naga City, Camarines Sur.

Raising the bar of academic excellence

STI College Naga's newest home takes pride in the latest state-of-the-art facilities housed in a 4-storey building that stands on a 5,000-square meter property. Accessible from all points in the Bicol Region, the STI Academic Center Naga aims to provide unparalleled education in Information and Communications Technology, Engineering, and Hospitality & Tourism Management.

The eager students of Bicol can now take advantage of STI Academic Center

STI College Naga's fully air-conditioned classrooms, computer laboratories with flat screen monitors, hot and cold kitchen areas, mock front office, dining area, and suite room for its Hotel and Restaurant Management students, and a library packed with the latest research materials. It also has a canteen, courtyard for students, and a spacious multi-purpose covered court.
